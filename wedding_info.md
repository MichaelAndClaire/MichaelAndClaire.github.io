## FAQs

### Travel

#### Closest airport?

The closest and cheapest airport is Denver International Airport, 1.5–2 hours away.

#### Do I need to get a rental car?

Not necessarily, but recommended. You can book shuttles from the Denver Airport to Estes Park, including to the Y and other hotels, and we will have a shuttle to and from the wedding spot. Uber, Lyft, and taxis are also available in Estes, and the national park has some shuttles. Still, a rental car is probably recommended to make exploring easier.

#### Will you have a hotel block?

No, we will not have a hotel block. However, there are many good options in and around Estes Park, with cheaper options in Longmont or Loveland, about 45 minutes away. Given the influx of visitors to the national park in the summer, we recommend booking lodging ASAP! Please see the Travel & Lodging tab for more recommendations and details.

#### Concerning elevation

Estes Park is just over 7,500 feet, and our wedding venue is about 8,700 feet above sea level. Elevation sickness is a potential risk. We recommend those who may be sensitive consider arriving a day or two early and getting acclimated in lower-elevation Front Range cities, such as Longmont or Boulder. Staying hydrated is also key!

### Wedding Day Logistics

#### Dress Code

Cocktail attire. Think a little bit fancy, a little bit mountain! We recommend a light jacket for after-sunset temperatures and practical shoes for walking up and down the slope to our outdoor chapel — and for dancing, ofc.

#### What about kids and plus-ones?

We love kids and all your plus ones; however, due to wedding size restrictions, we had to make some tough calls. Please see your wedding invitation for specifics or contact us!

#### Can I drive to the wedding?

There is limited parking at the actual Mountainside Lodge, and we will reserve parking for those who need it for accessibility reasons. Please reach out! We will have a shuttle between a main parking lot at the YMCA, exact one TBD, and the Mountainside Lodge.

#### What time should I arrive?

Please plan to arrive 30 minutes before the ceremony. Shuttles will be timed accordingly.

#### Open bar?

We may be getting married at a YMCA, but we are gonna throw down.

#### You’re getting married outside? What about possible bad weather?

The wedding will stay outside if it’s not heavily raining. Please bring an umbrella and/or a rain jacket! If the weather is really bad, we will move inside.
